# Vanish Box

A lightweight desktop workspace utility for keeping active task materials organized — files, notes, sketches, and links — all in one place, all local, no account required.

---

## Download

**[→ Download the latest installer](https://github.com/spirtexfer/vanish-box/releases/latest)**

Run the `.exe`, click through the installer, and you're done.

---

## What it does

Vanish Box sits in your system tray and opens instantly when you need it. Inside, you organize everything by **tabs** — one tab per task or project.

Each tab holds:
- **Files** — drag files in from anywhere; they're copied into the app and stay there until you remove them
- **Notes** — quick text cards with a title and body; click to edit
- **Sketches** — freehand canvas cards; draw rough diagrams or wireframes without opening a heavy tool
- **Links** — named URLs that open in your browser with one click

## How to use

### Opening the app
- Press **Ctrl+Shift+J** (default) to toggle the panel from anywhere
- Or click the **Vanish Box icon in your system tray**

### Tabs
- Click **+** to create a new tab — give it a name, pick a color, and optionally choose a template (blank, research, coding, planning)
- **Double-click** a tab name to rename it
- **Drag** tabs left/right to reorder them
- Click **×** on a tab to delete it (requires confirmation; last tab cannot be deleted)
- Click **⊘** in the header to clear all content from the active tab (undoable)

### Files
- **Drag and drop** files from Windows Explorer onto the panel
- Click a filename to **open it** with its default app
- **×** removes the file from the app (file stays on your disk)
- **🗑** permanently deletes the file from your computer (moves to Recycle Bin)
- **Drag** file cards up/down to reorder them

### Notes
- Click **+ Add note** to create a note card
- Click the card to **open the editor** — edit the title and body, then save
- **×** deletes the note (an undo toast appears for 4 seconds)
- **▼/▶** collapses or expands the body preview
- **Drag** note cards to reorder them

### Sketches
- Click **+ Add sketch** to create a sketch card
- Click the card to **open the canvas editor**
  - Switch between **pen** and **eraser** tools
  - Adjust **brush size** with the slider
  - **Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y** or the ↩/↪ buttons to undo/redo strokes
  - Click **Clear** to wipe the canvas (with confirmation)
- **×** deletes the sketch (undoable for 4 seconds)
- **Drag** sketch cards to reorder them

### Links
- Click **+ Add link** to save a URL with an optional title
- Click the **link title** to open it in your browser
- **✎** edits the link; **×** removes it (undoable for 4 seconds)
- **Drag** link cards to reorder them

### Moving items between tabs
- Each item has a **Move** button to send it to another tab

### Command Palette
- Press **Ctrl+K** to open the command palette
- Quickly create tabs, notes, sketches, or links — or search across all your content

### Settings
- Click **⚙** in the header to open settings
- Toggle **dark mode**, show/hide file size and timestamp
- Click the **keybind field** and press a new combination to change the global shortcut

---

## Notes

- Everything is stored **locally** — no cloud, no account, no internet connection required
- Files are copied into app-managed storage; removing a file from Vanish Box never touches your original unless you use the 🗑 delete action
- The app lives in your system tray and doesn't appear in the taskbar
